Company

Mycroft have a team of fully trained and uniformed commissionaires.
Frank Stanley, who is well known for his honesty and integrity
within the corporate world, founded the company. Frank has been
a commissionaire for over 12 years. He has a military background,
serving as a section commander in the 1st Battalion of the Duke of
Edinburgh's Royal Regiment (Berkshire & Wiltshire). He is now
managing director of Mycroft and uses his experience in security
by advising corporate and private customers in addition to assisting
at charity events.
